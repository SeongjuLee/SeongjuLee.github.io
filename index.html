About Me

Ph.D Candidate in AI Convergence

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D in Artificial Intelligence at Gwangju Institute of Science and Technology (GIST), one of South Korea's premier research institutions. My work focuses on developing novel AI algorithms and models that push the boundaries of machine learning and cognitive computing.

Under the guidance of Professor Kyoobin Lee, I am exploring cutting-edge techniques in deep learning, computer vision, and neural network architectures. My research aims to bridge the gap between theoretical AI advancements and practical applications that can benefit society.
